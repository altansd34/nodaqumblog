Meet 'AGT All-Stars' Beatbox Group Berywam

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, August 7, 2024

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent America's Got Talent: All-Stars

Photo by: Casey Durkin/NBC

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Viral beatboxing champions Berywam has made their way back to the AGT stage for the chance to be crowned All-Stars champions. The French beatbox crew has wowed the judges and the audience with their synchronized beatboxing performance.

They Are French Beatbox Champions

The group, formed in Toulouse, France, has been competing professionally since 2016. Soon after their creation they won the French Beatbox Championships in 2016 and qualified for the World Championships, which they then won in Berlin in 2018. Berywam expertly combines rap and beatbox voices in a highly energetic live performance where they perform standout original songs and memorable covers.

Beatness, Rythmind, Wawad, and Beasty are four talented guys who cover popular songs and post them on social media, where they quickly gain popularity. The name of the band is a combination of the names of the four initial members: Beatness, Rythmind, Wawad, and MB14. In 2018, MB14 left the group, and Beasty joined completing the current lineup of Berywam today.

After earning a contract with Barclay Records, which is now owned by Universal Music Group, Berywam released their debut EP in 2017. The group has performed as the opener for musicians including Bigflo & Oli and Georgio. By 2018, Berywam had appeared on German television and then gained popularity online for their various song covers. They also received an invitation to take part in the renowned Les Francofolies de La Rochelle music festival in 2018.

For those who may not remember, Berywam first appeared on America’s Got Talent in season 14. They finished as a quarterfinalist.

They Have Done Many Stage Performances

The French beatboxing group Berywam made waves on several venues in the summer of 2022. Notably, the group performed at the Ronquières Festival for more than 65,000 people.

More recently, Berywam played at a concert in Brussels, Belgium on December 15. They are also going on a 2023 tour called the No Instrument Tour. The tour kicks off on March 11 in Cornebarrieu, France. Tickets are now available, prices start at €10 on Berywam’s official website.

Berywam are the current champions of beatboxing and they are a group to look out for in the upcoming AGT All–Stars. The premiere will air on January 2, 2023 on Peacock and NBC.
