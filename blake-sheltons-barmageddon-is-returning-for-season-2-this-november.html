Blake Shelton's 'Barmageddon' is Returning For Season 2 This November

Posted by Patria Henriques on Sunday, August 18, 2024

TALENTRECAP.COM More Shows

Eric Ogden/USA Network

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Blake Shelton is set to make his return to TV as part of his hit game show, Barmageddon. The show, that’s gearing up for its second season, is co-hosted by Shelton, The Voice‘s Carson Daly, and WWE Hall of Famer Nikki Garcia. The show will once again take place in the heart of the country star’s Ole Red bar in Nashville.

Barmageddon Season 2 Premieres This Fall

Exciting news for fans of Shelton’s USA Network show, Barmageddon. The second season of this bar-game series is set to premiere on November 13. Get ready to be entertained by the hilarious antics, fun competitions, and surprise celebrity appearances in this much-anticipated return.

Don’t miss the happiest hour on television as Shelton, Daly, and Garcia serve up more laughter and excitement in Barmageddon Season 2. It’s the ultimate showdown where celebrities come together for a good time and a good cause, and the games return bigger and better than ever.

In each thrilling episode of Barmageddon, two celebrities will face off in a series of five bar games, making for an evening filled with laughter, competition, and camaraderie. What sets Barmageddon apart is that the victor of these games doesn’t just walk away with bragging rights. They also earn a prize for “a viral internet sensation,” each carefully selected to support a unique cause or individual.

The stage is set, the contestants are ready, and the laughter is guaranteed. Barmageddon Season 2 is on its way, and it promises to be even more entertaining and star-studded than before. Be sure to tune in on the fun this fall as Barmageddon returns on November 13 on the USA Network.

This Season Promises to Be Even Bigger Than Before

The show promises a blend of hilarious and challenging bar games, including Fool’s Ball, Tic Tac Throwdown, Talk Derby to Me, and the intriguing “Wheel of Redemption.” The latter is designed to give struggling contestants a chance at a dramatic comeback.

The second season is also set to be bigger and more star-studded, with a diverse array of celebrity guests. These include Carly Pearce, Jelly Roll, Gwen Stefani, and Kelly Clarkson, who are all gearing up for some unforgettable appearances.

Beyond the country music community, Barmageddon has enlisted an even more diverse lineup of contestants. Ranging from rapper Ice-T and R&B legends Boyz II Men to actors Michelle Rodriguez and also David Arquette.

Ice-T will add a holiday twist to the show with a special episode. He aims to transform the set into a winter wonderland for festive-themed games such as Merry Axe-Mas, Doodle All the Way, Little Drummer Boy (and Girl), Reindeer Games, and Christmas Carol-okie.
