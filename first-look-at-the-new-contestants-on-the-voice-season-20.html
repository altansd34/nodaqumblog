Fans of The Voice are excited for the Season 20 premiere which is just a few days away. Nick Jonas is making his big return as a coach joining Blake Shelton, Kelly Clarkson and John Legend who are all vying to win. Let’s meet some of the talented contestants who are ready to impress the judges on this exciting new season.

Contestants On ‘The Voice’ Season 20

Kenzie Wheeler

Kenzie Wheeler is a country boy from Dover, Florida with big dreams of becoming country music’s next superstar. He previously got to perform with ACM New Male Artist of the Year winner Riley Green. You can follow him on Instagram @kenziewheelermusic.

Emma Caroline

Emma Caroline is ready to bring her country singing roots to The Voice. The singer from Nashville released her album Pursue back in 2017 which debuted on the top 50 charts. You can follow her on Instagram @emmacarolinemusic.

Avery Roberson

Avery Roberson is a country artist who is no stranger to talent competition shows. He previously auditioned on the American Idol bus tour in Columbia, South Carolina in 2019. You can follow him on Instagram. @averyrobersonmusic.

Riolana Doyle

Riolana grew up performing in local theater productions like Frozen and Aladdin. The 16-year-old is ready to take her tremendous talents to The Voice. You can follow her on Instagram @riolana.doyle.

Gean Garcia

Gean Garcia is a 19-year-old singer who was featured in a promo for Season 20 of The Voice. It is during his audition that Nick Jonas accuses Blake Shelton of being a bully so definitely be on the lookout for this young talent. You can follow him on Instagram @geankymusic.

Ryleigh Modig

Ryleigh Modig is a star on the rise. She previously won TV station WCCA’s New Voices Award and shares glimpses of her stunning singing voice on TikTok. You can follow her on Instagram @ryleighmodig.

Connor Christian

Connor Christian is a newlywed who has a ton of experience playing gigs around Chicago. We are already predicting that Connor will be a major heartthrob on this season of The Voice. You can follow him @_connorchristian.

Ciana Pelekai

Ciana Pelekai previously competed on Season 4 of America’s Got Talent. The singer from Hawaii returned for Season 8 where she made it to the Quarterfinals. You can follow her on Instagram @officiallyciana.

Keegan Ferrell

Keegan Ferrell is a pop singer from Fort Wayne, Indiana that currently lives in Nashville. He grew up playing classical and jazz piano and definitely has all the skills necessary to win The Voice. You can follow him on Instagram @keeganferrellmusic.

Aaron Konzelman

Aaron Konzelman and his wife Amanda make up the singing duo The Union Revival. The pair met in college and have been singing together ever since. You can follow Aaron on Instagram @aaronkonzelman.

Ethan Lively

Country boy Ethan Lively is just 17 years old hoping to make it big. According to his website, Ethan is from a small town “no bigger than a flea on a dog.” You can follow him on Instagram @ethan_lively99.

Savanna Woods

Savanna Woods grew up in a musical family and wrote her first song at the age of 3. Not only has she made music a full-time job, but she also launched her own production company called “Wandering Woods Productions.” You can follow her on Instagram @savannawoodsmusic.

Anna Grace Felten

Anna Grace Felten is a 20-year-old artist with over 117,000 followers on TikTok. One of her videos has nearly one million views and we are sure that number will only increase once she showcases her angelic sound on The Voice. You can follow her on Instagram @anna.grace.music.

Gihanna Zoe

Gihanna Zoe is another budding TikTok star with over 108,000 followers on the platform. The young singer is obsessed with poetry and completely slays her Instagram pictures. You can follow her @gihannazoe.

Corey Ward

You might recognize Corey Ward after his show-stopping Season 19 audition. Well, the good news is that he is back and hoping for the coaches to turn their chairs this season. We have high hopes for him on The Voice Follow him on Instagram @corey_ward222.

Halley Greg

Singer-songwriter Halley Greg just had her song “Worth It” featured on the New Indie Gold Spotify playlist. She is no stranger to the stage but also has a career as a high school science teacher. You can follow her on Instagram @halleygregmusic.

JD Casper

JD Casper is based in Austin, Texas. He has been pursuing music full-time for over 5 years. Over the years he has learned how to balance music with being a father to his young son. You can follow him on Instagram @jd_casper.

Pia Renee

Pia Renee is a singer who is completely skilled at playing the bass. She previously auditioned for Season 7 of American Idol earning high praise from Simon Cowell. You can follow her on Instagram @iampiarenee.

Zae Romeo

Zae Romeo is a pop artist from McKinney, Texas. He struck up a great friendship with fellow contestant Rachel Mac so definitely keep an eye on these two young singers in the competition. You can follow him on Instagram @zaeromeo.

Raine Stern

Raine Stern is a singer-songwriter who also has a knack for poetry. She has had numerous experiences performing with bands but is ready to take her act solo on The Voice. You can follow her on Instagram @rainestern.

Devan Blake Jones

Devan Blake Jones has been singing since he was 6 years old. He previously competed on American Idol and has spent years becoming a master in the music business. You can follow him on Instagram @devanblakejones.

Awari

Awari is a fitness model and Air Force veteran. This single father of two is ready to take his unique vocals all the way on The Voice. You can follow him on Instagram @awari.official.

Bradley Sinclair

Bradley Sinclair currently lives in Nashville where he is pursuing a music career. He is a rockstar on the guitar and the piano and previously released Christian music. You can follow him on Instagram @bradleysinclairmusic.

Ainae

Ainae has a unique voice that combines R&B, hip hop and pop. The singer from Washington DC has a ton of on-stage experience and an infectious personality. You can follow her on Instagram @ainaemusix.

Pete Mroz

Pete Mroz describes himself as “a father, husband, lover of music, lover of people, songwriter, guitar player, photographer, videographer, business man and a bluesman.” He has written and recorded five albums and is hoping to become a star on The Voice. You can follow him on Instagram @petemroz.

Deion Warren

Deion Warren is a gospel singer whose incredible voice literally rivals coach John Legend. He announced he would be joining The Voice Season 20 on his late grandmother’s birthday, making it an emotional day for him. You can follow him on Instagram @deionqwarren.

Jordan Matthew Young

Jordan Matthew Young has already gotten to tour with some incredible artists like ZZ Top and Kyle Gass Band. The soulful singer is based out of Austin, Texas. You can follow him on Instagram @jordanmatthewyoung.

Christine Cain

Though Christine Cain is not a huge social media user, she does have one big voice. We cannot wait to see how she does in the competition. Follow her on Instagram @itschristinecain.

Cam Anthony

Cam Anthony won “Showtime at the Apollo” when he was 16. At the age of 12 he was signed to Dr. Dre’s Aftermath Records. He also performed on The Ellen DeGeneres Show that same year. You can follow him on Instagram @therealcamanthony.

Savanna Chestnut

Savanna Chestnut is a country artist from a small farm town in Kansas. She was previously nominated for Female Vocalist of the Year at the Rocky Mountain Country Music Awards twice. You can follow her on Instagram @savannachestnut.

Durell Anthony

Durell Anthony has been a background vocalist for some of the world’s biggest celebrities like American Idol winner Carrie Underwood. He is ready to take the leap as a solo singer on The Voice. You can follow him on Instagram @durellanthonysings.

Victor Solomon

Victor Solomon is a Christian artist ready to show the world his talent on The Voice. He already has music available on streaming platforms that you can check out here. You can follow him on Instagram @_victorsolo.

Zania Alake

Zania Alake is a singer who has fused R&B and jazz perfectly. She has prior experience working in stage productions as an actress. Above all, she takes pride in being a mom. You can follow her on Instagram @zaniaalake.

Carolina Rial

Carolina Rial is a pop artist with an already impressive resume at the age of 17. She has performed at Red Bull Arena, Marlins Park and MetLife Stadium along with making multiple television appearances. You can follow her on Instagram @carolinarialmusic.

Denisha Dalton

Denisha Dalton is a singer who pours her emotions into every song she takes on. She also has a YouTube channel where she posts makeup reviews and covers. You can follow her on Instagram @denishakdalton.

Dana Monique

Dana Monique is a background singer who grew up in a musical family. Dana’s audition was actually featured in a promo for The Voice where she shows off her powerhouse vocals. You can follow her on Instagram @thedanamonique.

Andrew Marshall

Andrew Marshall is a leukemia survivor. Music helped him through the intense battle and he is ready to share his talent with the world. You can follow him on Instagram @awmmusic.

Rachel Mac

Rachel Mac is a 15-year-old high school sophomore. She sings Indie and Folk music and has a voice well beyond her years. You can follow her on Instagram @rachelmac.

Jose Figueroa Jr.

Jose Figueroa Jr. is a gospel singer and a pastor who has been performing since he was 6 years old. He previously won the gospel edition of Apollo Amateur Night. You can follow him on Instagram @jfigueroajr.

Lindsay Joan

Lindsay Joan has been working in musical theater since she was 15 years old. She previously toured the country with the national tour of Kinky Boots. You can follow her on Instagram @lindsayjoanofficial.
