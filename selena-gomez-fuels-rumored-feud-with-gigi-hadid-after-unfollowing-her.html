Selena Gomez Fuels Rumored Feud With Gigi Hadid After Unfollowing Her

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, August 19, 2024

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

Getty Images for The Met Museum/Vogue

Reading Time: 2 minutes

The recent unfollowing spree of pop icon Selena Gomez and supermodel Gigi Hadid has sent shockwaves through the virtual world, fueling a rumored feud between the two. Gossip columns and tabloids have been quick to speculate on the motivations behind this social media activity, but there may be more to it than just simple drama.

Selena Gomez Unfollows Gigi, Bella Hadid

It all began with a single click. Gomez, a renowned musician, and actress, unfollowed Hadid, along with her sister Bella Hadid and the enigmatic Zayn Malik, on the popular photo-sharing platform Instagram.

The unfollowing spree, which occurred over the course of a weekend, ignited a flurry of speculation and rumors. Fans and observers couldn’t help but wonder what might have transpired to prompt such an act.

A delicate balance between curiosity and respect for privacy should be practiced. It is important to note that neither Gomez nor any of the parties involved have explicitly addressed the unfollowing saga. However, a trusted source close to Gomez has clarified that there is no ongoing feud or drama between the individuals.

Gomez and Hadid both share a deep bond with the talented Taylor Swift, forming a trifecta of friendship that has captivated fans worldwide. Their camaraderie was recently captured in photographs, as Swift and Hadid enjoyed a delightful dinner together in the vibrant city of New York.

The Drama Continued When Gomez Was Romantically Linked With Zayn Malik

The unfolding drama took an unexpected turn when rumors surfaced of a romantic link between Gomez and Zayn Malik. Reports claimed that the pair were spotted sharing a tender moment during a cozy dinner in New York City.

People whispered that Gomez and Malik were seen holding hands and sharing gentle kisses, suggesting a growing romance. However, since there were no photos or additional reports, these rumors remained uncertain.

When news of Gomez and Malik’s alleged romance broke, a source close to Hadid shared her indifference towards their relationship. Focused on co-parenting their beloved two-year-old daughter Khai, Hadid’s stance was clear. As long as Malik is happy, stable, and maintains a loving relationship with their child, she supports his choices wholeheartedly.
