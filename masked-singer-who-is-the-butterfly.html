The Masked Singer season 2 is starting a whole new guessing game! The clues are harder than before and the celebrities are bigger than ever, The Butterfly was the first to step onto the stage, sporting a costume covered in sequins and larger-than-life wings.

Here are the clues and performances from the Butterfly so far.

Clues On ‘The Masked Singer’

In the first episode, the Butterfly says that she has been waiting patiently for the past year for her “metamorphosis.” She is seen on a photo shoot as she talks about thriving in different fields. Is it possible she has had a career in modeling? Then, she says that London used to be home to her. The Butterfly ends by saying she’s here to “take us to church” and is seen praying. When asked, she tells the judges she has “a lot of soul in her wings.”

Popular Guesses On ‘The Masked Singer’

The judges agreed that the Butterfly must be a professional singer. Nicole guessed Cara DeLevingne and Diana Ross, then Jenny predicted it’s Fantasia or Mel B. Many fans online are guessing Michelle Williams, Victoria Beckham, Rita Ora, and Mel B. But by far it seems like the most popular prediction is Michelle Williams, with many fans claiming they “know” it’s her.

Our Prediction And Possible Spoilers

From the clues, our prediction is Michelle Williams. Multiple times the Butterfly references faith and praying. Michelle grew up religious and made her musical debut in a church. She’s also sung with gospel groups in the past. The clues also mention being involved in many fields. Michelle has done acting, dancing, and Broadway in addition to her singing career. She also has ties to London, which the clues alluded to. The singer had performed at the West End Theatre and also competed on the UK series, Strictly Come Dancing. But more than anything, the Butterfly’s voice just sounds like Michelle Williams!
