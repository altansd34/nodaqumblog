Every human being has a pre-ordained path in life. We often think hard about what lies in our future. Do we have control over it or not? When you feel a little blue about life, these destiny quotes will remind you to remain positive and to take charge of your life.

What does destiny really mean? It refers to what is intended to be and what is written in the stars. Many people think that it is a divine decree. What decides our future and fate? Our decisions determine what becomes of our lives, and we have the power to control it as many destiny quotes remind us.

Top destiny quotes on life and love

Is there fate or destiny? Yes, there is. Fate comes from the word fatum, which means "things that have already been uttered." It usually has a negative connotation in society.
