Who is Alexandra Grant? Bio of Keanu Reeves' new partner

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, August 23, 2024

Alexandra Grant is an artist who probes ideas of identity, social responsibility, and translation, among others, through the use of language and text in various media. Based in Los Angeles, she frequently collaborates with other philosophers, writers, and artists.

Growing up, Alexandra Grant was raised in different countries such as Spain, France, and Mexico. Her work is fueled by the need to understand how the images we see and languages we speak influence how we think and exchange ideas. She also seeks to answer how writers and artists can influence culture in a rapidly growing technology-based world.

Grant was born in Fairview Park, Ohio, in 1973. Her parents, a political science professor and a Scottish geology professor, were fond of travelling to different parts of the world. Unfortunately, they divorced. The artist's mother got custody of her daughter.

Career

Alexandra's first solo exhibition took place in 2007. This occurred at the Museum of Contemporary Art in L.A. Some people describe her work as a radical collaboration as she collaborates with writers, linguists, philosophers, and other artists to create most of her artistic designs.
