A Grandma of 17 Married to a Man 37 Years Her Junior Plans to Have a Baby Despite All Naysayers

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, August 26, 2024

Despite being almost 4 decades older than him, 62-year-old Cheryl McGregor is happily married to 25-year-old Quran McCain. The 2 started dating only a few years ago, but they’re both convinced that they finally found the person they’ve been searching for. McGregor is a grandmother of 17, but she and McCain are also planning to have children together no matter what their family members or strangers on the Internet may think.

Cheryl McGregor met Quran McCain through her son.

Quran McCain and Cheryl McGregor’s love story began when they met at a restaurant where McCain was employed as a worker under McGregor’s son, who was his manager. However, they did not have any significant interaction at that time. It was only after several years when they reunited at a store, where McGregor worked as a cashier, that they decided to keep in touch.

She’s a grandma of 17, and they have a 37-year age gap, but this didn’t prevent them from falling in love.

Initially, they were just friends, but eventually, they acknowledged the mutual romantic attraction between them and started dating. At this time, McGregor was 60, a mom of 7, and a grandma of 17. But McCain, while only 23 years old, revealed that they “connected on a spiritual level.” And it only took one date to convince him that he had feelings for McGregor.

They got engaged after a year of dating and tied the knot in 2021.

After only one year of dating, the lovebirds decided to take the next step in their relationship and got engaged. And that same year, they tied the knot as well. But despite the couple’s determination to be together no matter what, they had to face many struggles, including McGregor’s family’s disapproval.

“Nobody was accepting, everyone thought we were joking or thought it was all games,” McCain revealed about his relationship with McGregor. On the other hand, McCain’s family quickly grew to love his bride and soon accepted her.

A few years back, they started posting videos on TikTok and quickly went viral.

McGregor and McCain’s story is known to netizens due to their viral TikTok videos that they started posting a couple of years ago. In the short clips, the couple is usually seen dancing to trending songs or addressing questions and criticism they receive about their relationship. Despite receiving several hate comments over the years, McGregor’s TikTok account has almost 4 million followers.

Despite the hateful comments they receive, the couple’s love only grows stronger by the day.

Throughout their entire relationship, McGregor and McCain have had to face backlash from family and strangers alike. But this only helped their bond grow even stronger. “It’s different, it’s amazing, he’s very compassionate to me and I think it’s because he’s a younger man,” McGregor gushed about her husband. “There’s just something between him and me that I never thought we would be together like this, he’s just an amazing person,” she added.

Their biggest dream is to have a family together one day.

McGregor and McCain’s next goal together is to start a family. Due to McGregor’s age and the age difference between them, this idea was met with criticism and ridicule from their followers. But the couple is determined to welcome a baby and plans to hire a surrogate to finally make their biggest dream become reality.
