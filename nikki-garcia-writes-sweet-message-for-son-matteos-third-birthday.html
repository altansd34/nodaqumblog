Nikki Garcia Writes Sweet Message for Son Matteos Third Birthday

Posted by Patria Henriques on Sunday, August 18, 2024

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

Photo by Jason Mendez/Getty Images

Reading Time: 2 minutes

WWE Superstar Nikki Garcia recently celebrated her son Matteo’s third birthday. In addition to the celebration, the tv personality penned an emotional message on Instagram, for her and Artem Chigvinstsev’s toddler.

Nikki Garcia Celebrates Son Matteo’s Third Birthday

On July 31, Garcia shared a compilation of Matteo. The video is set to make anyone emotional, since Taylor Swift’s “Never Grow Up” plays as background music.

Alongside the video, Garcia wrote an adorable message for her three-year-old. Within the message, she described Matteo as her “sweet baby boy, forever mommy’s baby boy.”

“Oh Matteo I can’t believe you are 3 today how happy and emotional that makes me,” she wrote. “One day Mommy will tell how you changed her life in the most incredible ways. Sacrifice has been nothing but a positive in promising you that I would raise you right and be there for almost every moment in your life.”

The WWE star went on to state that Matteo has brought her “so much love” and makes her happy. She then promised to protect him forever.

“Grateful to God everyday for putting your Daddy in my life so we can have you. Here’s to 3 being just as magical as 1 & 2!!! I love you Tay Tay!!! Forever and always,” she wrote.

Bonita Bonita Wine Instagram page, owned by the Garcia twins, also took the time to wish Matteo a happy birthday. The page then revealed that Matteo is featured on their Chardonnay labels.

Matteo Forms “Perfect Little Tag Team” with Brie Garcia’s Son Buddy

In the video compilation that Garcia uploaded, it can be noticed that most of Matteo’s moments were taped while he’s with his cousin, Buddy. For those who may not know, Matteo and Buddy were born within 22 hours of each other.

It’s thus fair to say that the two are not only relatives but also the best of friends. A year ago, Nikki and Brie talked about the adorable bonding that their sons have formed as babies.

“We have been used to these wild boys,” Brie stated. “It’s funny because they are already a perfect little tag team. I feel like it’s baby mania every day at the house. Whether Buddy is head butting someone or they’re cuddling each other. It’s really cute, the relationship. We always look at them and we are like, ‘They act like brothers.'”

Nikki Garcia and Chigvintsev welcomed Matteo on July 31, 2020. Less than a day after, Buddy was born, making Brie Garcia and Bryan Danielson parents to two.
