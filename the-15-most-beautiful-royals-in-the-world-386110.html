The15 Most Beautiful Royals inthe World

Posted by Patria Henriques on Sunday, August 25, 2024

Royal people always attract attention, especially beautiful ones.

Bright Side couldn’t resist sharing a list of these gorgeous princes and princesses who, moreover, are intelligent, talented, helpful, and very noble.

Queen Rania of Jordan

She married King Abdullah II on June 10, 1993, and now has 4 children. She is known for her involvement in philanthropic, charitable, and cultural activities. Queen Rania uses online social networks such as YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter.

Diana, Princess of Wales

Lady Diana Spencer married Prince Charles in July 1981 and was the mother of Princes William and Harry. Diana was known for her charity work as well as for her style. She died in a car crash in Paris on August 31, 1997, but she still remains a fashion icon and the “queen of people’s hearts.”

Crown Princess Mary of Denmark

She is the wife of Frederik, Crown Prince of Denmark. Their official engagement and their marriage was the subject of extensive attention from the media, who called it a modern "fairy-tale" romance between a prince and a commoner. Mary was voted Woman of the Year 2008 by a Danish magazine. The Princess donated her cash reward to charity.

Crown Princess Masako of Japan

Masako Owada first met Naruhito, Crown Prince of Japan, during her studies at the University of Tokyo. The prince was immediately captivated by her, but Masako refused to marry the prince at first because it would force her to give up her career in diplomacy and restrict her freedoms. She finally accepted his third proposal on December 9, 1992.

Maharani Gayatri Devi of Jaipur

Educated in Europe, the Maharani was a striking beauty in her youth and grew up to become quite a fashion icon. Passionate about horse riding, she was an able polo player and a good shot, often indulging in hunting, a royal pastime, in her youth. She dedicated her whole life to philanthropic efforts.

Prince Philippos of Greece and Denmark

Prince Philippos is the 5th and youngest child of King Constantine II and Queen Anne-Marie of Greece. He graduated from Georgetown University and currently works in New York City with hedge funds.

Princess Madeleine of Sweden

Princess Madeleine is married to British-born American financier Christopher O’Neill. They have 2 children, a daughter, Princess Leonore, and a son, Prince Nicolas. It was announced on August 27, 2017, that they are expecting their third child in March 2018.

Andrea Casiraghi

Andrea is the eldest grandchild of Rainier III, Prince of Monaco, and American actress Grace Kelly. In 2008, Forbes placed him 10th on their "20 Hottest Young Royals" list. Casiraghi is fluent in several languages. He loves sports, especially skiing, football, horse riding, and water sports. His hobby is collecting Swatch watches.

Grace Kelly

Grace was an American film actress who, after marrying Prince Rainier III, became Princess of Monaco and has topped a poll of the most beautiful royals of all time. She died tragically after having been in a car accident on September 14, 1982. Her beauty and style became legendary, and she is still an example to follow for many women.

Queen Letizia

The beauty of the Spanish royal family. She was a professional journalist and news anchor but left it all behind when she married into royalty. Letizia and King Felipe VI have 2 daughters: Leonor, born in 2005, and Infanta Sofía, born in 2007.

Princess Ameerah of Saudi Arabia

This Saudi Arabian princess and philanthropist was raised by her divorced mother and her grandparents. At age 18, she met Prince Al-Waleed bin Talal while conducting an interview for a school paper. They eventually married, but they divorced in November 2013. Princess Ameerah graduated from the University of New Haven with a degree in Business Administration.

Prince Carl Philip of Sweden

He lives in central Stockholm with his wife, Princess Sofia, and 2 sons, Prince Alexander, born in 2016, and Prince Gabriel, born in 2017. Prince Carl Philip is very athletic and enjoys sports such as swimming, sailing, football, and skiing. He is also interested in design and drawing and even studied it.

Crown Prince Hamdan of Dubai

Sheikh Hamdan is a famous Instagram user with 5.7 million followers. He shares pictures of his active and adventurous life as a traveler, semi-professional skydiver, diver, and horse rider. He also writes poems, which are well known and are mainly romantic, patriotic, and about his family.

Catherine, Duchess of Cambridge

When Kate Middleton married Prince William on April 29, 2011, she not only became royalty but one of the most beautiful royals in history. The Duchess became prominent for her fashion style and has been placed on numerous "best dressed" lists. She is also famous for her charity work.

Charlotte Casiraghi

Charlotte Marie Pomeline Casiraghi is a granddaughter of Rainier III, Prince of Monaco, and American actress Grace Kelly. She is the official equestrian ambassador of the Gucci label, and her clothes are exclusively designed by Frida Giannini, the creative director of Gucci.
