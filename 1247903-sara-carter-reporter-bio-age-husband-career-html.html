What is Sara Carter doing now?

Sara is a Fox News Channel contributor and investigative reporter. Sara Carter's Fox News career began in 2017. She was also a reporter at The Washington Times and The Washington Examiner.

In her work, she unravelled the existing secret tunnel systems, the routes used to traffic narcotics, and the role of federal officials in Mexico in drug trading. She has interviewed many foreign officials and heads of state. She has also led discussions on critical issues such as national security.
