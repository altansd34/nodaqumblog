Kelsea Ballerini Debuts New Hairstyle That Got Boyfriend Chase Stokes' Attention

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, August 19, 2024

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

Getty Images for The Recording Academy

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Country music superstar Kelsea Ballerini has surprised fans with a striking new look on social media. Known for her signature long blonde waves, the “If You Go Down (I’m Goin’ Down Too)” singer-songwriter decided to take a daring step and embrace a chic wavy lob hairstyle.

Kelsea Ballerini Unveils New Hairstyle

On Instagram, Ballerini shared a carousel of images showcasing her fresh haircut. The choppy and chic wavy lob beautifully framed her face, cascading gracefully down to her shoulders. Her radiant smile and confident demeanor exuded elegance and charm, leaving her followers in awe of her dramatic transformation.

The post’s caption read, “Out with the old, you get in with the new,” certainly hinting at the significance of this change in her life. Credit was also given to Nashville hairstylist Marissa Martin, who skillfully crafted the stunning look that perfectly complements Ballerini’s features.

This new hairstyle represents more than just a change in appearance for Ballerini. In a recent interview, she spoke about her journey of self-discovery and embracing her womanhood. The singer shared her newfound openness to exploring different aspects of herself, and also breaking free from the confines of her previous image.

Chase Stokes Reacts To Ballerini’s New Hairstyle

Ballerini’s new look didn’t go unnoticed by her fans and fellow celebrities. The post received an outpouring of love and support, with comments flooding in to applaud her for the bold and empowering choice.

Many of her followers were quick to compliment her, leaving comments like “This is summer hair goals!” and “You look incredible.” The positive reactions affirmed that her decision to step into her womanhood and embrace change resonated with many.

Even her boyfriend, Outer Banks actor Chase Stokes, chimed in with playful affection, writing, “I’m fine I’m fine I’m not fine.” It’s evident that the transformation struck a chord with him as well. Fellow musicians and celebrities also couldn’t resist expressing their admiration. T.J. Osborne of the duo Brothers Osborne left a simple kiss face emoji, and musician Betty Who added, “i loveee.”

Laguna Beach alum Kristin Cavallari joined the chorus of adoration, leaving a cheerful “Cuteeee!” to celebrate Ballerini’s fresh style. Interestingly, Ballerini’s influence extended beyond admiration. Her post inspired some of her followers to consider their own hair transformations.
