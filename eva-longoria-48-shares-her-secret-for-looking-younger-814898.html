Eva Longoria, 48, Shares Her SECRET for Looking Younger

Posted by Tandra Barner on Sunday, August 18, 2024

Eva Longoria is a triple threat in the entertainment industry, as an actress, producer and director. But she’s also popular for her beauty and ability to look young, no matter how many years go by. Fortunately, she decided to reveal the reason why she’s able to always look as if time is frozen for her.

In an interview, the Desperate Housewives-star was questioned about what tricks she might have that help her look 10 years younger. She didn’t hold back and gave a surprising answer.

“People think the trick to looking younger is makeup, but it’s skin-care,” Longoria said. “If you don’t have good skin, piling on makeup just makes you look older and much worse. When it comes to makeup, I don’t overset or over powder it. The more powder you put on the fine lines in your face, the more you see it. Most people say ’oh let me cover it’ but it looks much thicker and worse.”

But that’s not all. The actress also talked about how essential protecting your skin from the sun is, adivising people to apply foundation with SPF daily. “My mother was so strict on wearing sunblock,” she recalled. “She always said, ’the skin you take care of in your 20s is the skin you have in your 40s’.”

In a different interview, Longoria took time yet again to emphasize the fact that she never leaves the house without sun protection. “I wear sunblock every day and that’s been life-changing for my skin, just protecting it from sunspots and even driving in your car, you can be affected by the sun,” she shared.
