American Idol winner Chayce Beckham already has a pretty sweet gig lined up for later this year. The singer will be performing on tour with the rock band Three Doors Down.

Beckham recently walked away from the Season 19 competition with the winner’s title. His original song “23,” which he released as part of his time on the show, was a hit on the iTunes charts.

Chayce Beckham to Tour with Three Doors Down

On Wednesday, Beckham shared the news of his tour gig on social media. He’ll be joining the rock band Three Doors Down at three stops this September for the Better Life 20th Anniversary Tour. His first stop will be East Moline, Illinois, followed by Allegan, Michigan, and finally St. Louis, Missouri.

The band also posted the tour dates on Instagram, along with dates for five other acts who will be joining them on different parts of the tour — Seether, Candlebox, Theory, Blackberry Smoke, and Koe Wetzel. You can buy tickets here.

Beckham previously had the chance to duet with some other big names during his time on the show, including Luke Combs and Brandon Boyd. The 24-year-old singer has already been getting back into live performances since winning the show. Earlier this week, he performed at the Whiskey Jam at Winners Bar in Nashville.

Live Music is Returning At Last

A lot of live music was canceled or postponed in the last year due to the COVID-19 pandemic. In recent weeks, a number of artists have announced upcoming tours and invited fans to join them for their first live performances in months.

That includes several talent show stars, including The Voice coaches John Legend and Nick Jonas (with the Jonas Brothers), who are both heading out on tours later this year.

American Idol Season 17 winner Laine Hardy is also touring this summer, and Idol judge Luke Bryan recently announced that Crash My Playa, his concert vacation in Cancún, Mexico, will be returning in 2022. There’s a lot to look forward to in the coming months.
