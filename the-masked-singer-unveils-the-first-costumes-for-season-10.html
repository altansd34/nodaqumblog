The Masked Singer has revealed the first two costumes for Season 10 of the show, which is set to premiere in September. The show’s social media shared photos of Donut and Hawk, and they’re sporting some pretty elaborate outfits.

The Masked Singer Reveals Donut, Hawk Costumes

Donut’s costume actually features several donuts put together. The face is a pink donut with blue frosting, and the body is a donut with sprinkles and a bite taken out of it. The character’s arms and legs are also covered in donuts. The costume is topped off with a chef’s hat.

Hawk has a much more serious costume, featuring huge golden wings and a blue helmet. The character has clawed feet and hands, and a realistic-looking bird face. Hawk appears to have a pretty muscular upper body, which could be a hint about the celebrity underneath.

Fans in the comments of the show’s Instagram post had plenty to say about the new costumes, with one user writing that Donut’s eyes are “horrifying.” Another user wrote that it’s “one of the most disturbing costumes that show has had.”

People are also speculating about who might be under the costumes, with one user guessing that Hawk is John Cena. Someone else predicted that Hawk will be a great singer.

Season 10 Premieres September 27 on FOX

The Masked Singer will return for Season 10 on Wednesday, September 27. FOX has said this season features “the biggest and boldest costumes in the show’s history – with the buzziest and brightest celebrities underneath.”

The entire panel (Robin Thicke, Jenny McCarthy, Ken Jeong, and Nicole Scherzinger) will be back for the new season, and Nick Cannon will be back as host. This cast has stayed the same since the show premiered in 2019.

A previous teaser for Season 10 gave fans a peek at a few of the new costumes, including Donut. Another costume looks to have flower petals, and there’s also a character with wings that looks like it could be a moth.
