Each season of America’s Got Talent seems to be getting more and more focused on contestants’ sob stories. Over the years, fans have created memes and have taken their frustrations out on social media. Here are some of the most hilarious memes and posts we found after Tuesday’s AGT season premiere.

Social Media Explodes with ‘America’s Got Talent’ Sob Story Memes

Now in its 16th season, America’s Got Talent is sure to bring on more tearful stories, contestants with tragic life experiences, and things that will make the judges want to hit their Golden Buzzers.

1. The judge’s when they hear a kid singer describing their backstory

2. Contestants describing accidents they have had in the past that weaves into their act

3. The key to getting a Golden Buzzer: sing and have a sad backstory

4. The hilarious ‘You’re So Brave’ theme

5. Without tragic backstories, contestants just get red buzzers

6. Gold confetti falls for more contestants with tragic backstories than those who just simply perform their act

7. When an America’s Got Talent interview package starts off with someone describing their childhood and what has led them to the stage

8. If you’re going for gold, you probably should strut onto the America’s Got Talent stage like a model

9. ‘America’s love a good sob story’

10. The sob stories usually correlate to the contestants making it to the next round of the competition
