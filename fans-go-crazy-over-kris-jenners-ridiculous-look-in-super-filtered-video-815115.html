Its Not Your Face, Kris Jenner Slammed After Posting a Makeup Video

Posted by Billy Koelling on Sunday, August 18, 2024

Kris Jenner broke the internet with a video from her makeup artist, showing off her incredibly youthful look. Fans were amazed, but some called it “ridiculous,” sparking a debate about beauty standards and personal choices.

She had achieved a stunning figure at 67

Rich Fury / Invision / AP / East News

According to RadarOnline, Kris Jenner has gained almost 80 pounds in the last four months, with recent photographs of the reality star in a bikini showing her expanding body. Unfortunately, this has led to criticism from both fans and the media. However, Kris Jenner has displayed admirable resilience in the face of this negativity and has vowed to take control of her health and lose weight. Let us support her in her journey towards a healthier lifestyle.

Some fans have accused Kris Jenner of using Ozempic to achieve her “skinny” new figure, although she has not confirmed or denied these accusations. Regardless of these rumors, it is clear that Kris Jenner is dedicated to achieving her health goals and getting back in shape. Let us support her in her journey towards a healthier lifestyle and focus on the positive steps she takes to improve her well-being.

The matriarch of the Kardashian-Jenner family has recently been in the headlines for her weight loss journey. According to Bright Side, Kris Jenner has debuted a drastic weight loss and convinced fans that she’s aging in reverse.

This is a testament to Kris Jenner’s commitment to her health and well-being. She has not let the negativity get to her and has remained committed to losing weight, getting back in shape, and being her best self.

In a recent post by the 67-year-old matriarch of the Kardashian-Jenner family, she can be seen sporting a skintight leather-looking top and matching pants, showcasing her exceptionally fit physique. Notably, the author points out the unusual sight of Kris Jenner’s arms, which are typically kept covered, particularly in public. As one comment aptly observes, “I don’t think I’ve seen her arms, like ever.”

Her heavily filtered video sparked many controversies among fans

In the age of social media and celebrity culture, the line between reality and artifice can sometimes blur, leading to unexpected reactions from fans. A recent incident involving Kris Jenner’s makeup artist, Samer Khouzami, underscores how perception can be altered through the lens of technology and editing.

In this case, a heavily-filtered video posted on Instagram by Samer Khouzami showcased Kris Jenner, the matriarch of the renowned Kardashian family, in a state of glamorous transformation. However, instead of basking in the glow of Jenner’s beauty regimen, fans grappled with a sense of confusion and disbelief. Rather than getting compliments and admiration, the video led to an unexpected discussion surrounding the extent of the filters and alterations applied to Jenner’s appearance.

In an era where photo-editing tools and filters have become ubiquitous, it’s not uncommon for celebrities and individuals to use such tools to enhance their online presence. However, in the Instagram post’s comments section, a common sentiment emerged as multiple users pondered whether the video was a product of AI (Artificial Intelligence).

One follower wittily remarked, “The AI of Kris Jenner,” and others wrote, “Embrace your age. This filter is ridiculous.” But comments continued with remarks like, “What is happening that’s not her face!”

The way people reacted to the video shows that there’s a bigger discussion about how technology affects how we see things. But beyond the negative comments, some folks liked what she did, and they wrote something like: “Why add a filter? Kris is stunning. Wrinkles are not a bad thing, I promise.”

People have been questioning how Kris Jenner achieved her new look, but her family has dealt with critics before. As the writer mentions, Kris’s youngest daughter, Kylie, recently shut down rumors about having extensive plastic surgery. Looking at how attractive the whole family is, it’s pretty clear that Kris’s genes have a lot of power.
