Reba McEntire Advocates for LGBTQ Rights, Says She's 'Disappointed' of Tennessee's Anti-Drag Laws

Posted by Patria Henriques on Monday, August 19, 2024

TALENTRECAP.COM Singing The Voice

Photo by Taylor Hill/FilmMagic

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Country music legend, Reba McEntire, has made headlines for speaking out against Tennessee’s anti-drag laws. In a recent interview, McEntire expressed her disappointment in the state’s legislation, which prohibits individuals from wearing clothing that is not typically associated with their gender.

Reba McEntire is Against Anti-Drag Laws

McEntire, who has been a long-time advocate for the LGBTQ+ community, recently voiced her support of drag performers. In addition, she voiced that she believes they deserve the right to express themselves freely.

“God bless ’em for wearing those heels,” she said, referring to the often-high-heeled shoes worn by drag queens.

McEntire’s comments were praised by fans and activists, who see her as a powerful ally in the fight for equality. The country singer is not alone, many others have also criticized the laws as discriminatory and harmful to the LGBTQ+ community.

Her outspoken support for the LGBTQ+ community and her willingness to speak out on important issues has cemented her status as a role model for fans and also aspiring artists alike. As she takes the stage at the Hollywood Bowl, it’s clear that her impact on the industry will continually grow.

Reba McEntire Headlined the Iconic Hollywood Bowl

As a trailblazing country artist, McEntire’s impact extends far beyond her activism. She has paved the way for countless women in the industry. Singing songs that speak to the experiences of women and challenging gender norms in a male-dominated genre.

McEntire’s has an impressive list of accomplishments, including six Country Music Association. Awards, four consecutive wins for female vocalist of the year, and even opening a restaurant in her native Oklahoma.

Most recently, McEntire headlined the iconic Hollywood Bowl, a long-awaited accomplishment for the 68-year-old singer and actor. Her willingness to take risks and push boundaries has been a hallmark of her career, whether it’s through her music, or advocacy. Through it all, McEntire remains true to herself, using her platform to speak out on issues that matter to her.
