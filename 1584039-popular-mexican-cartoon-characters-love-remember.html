1. Diego Marquez (Go, Diego, Go!)

Diego Marquez is the main character of the preschool series Go, Diego, Go. He is a young action-adventure hero who loves nature and adventure. Diego loves animals and is determined to help those in trouble. He encourages people to love and protect their environment.

Garcia is the main character of Handy Manny. He is always happy, kind, and friendly, and he helps everyone with their problems in Sheetrock Hills. He is a handyman and can fix anything using his eight talking tools. He loves his profession and does it with love and passion. He loves a girl named Kelly, who owns a hardware store with the materials Manny needs for his projects.

3. Speedy Gonzales (Looney Tunes)

Speedy Gonzales is also one of the Mexican cartoons loved by fans. He is known for his lightning-fast speed and charming accent. He is portrayed as the fastest mouse in all of Mexico. His ability to run extremely fast and his exaggerated Mexican accent make him a popular Mexican cartoon character among fans.

He is mischievous, intelligent and heroic. Speedy Gonzales loves his town and defends it from his enemies. He is humorous and leaves audiences laughing because of his Mexican accent and personality.

He supports the twins Dipper and Mabel's pursuits and ensures they are always safe. He is naturally humorous and childish, making him a unique character.

What is the oldest Mexican cartoon?

The oldest Mexican cartoon is Mi Sueno. It was made in 1915; however, the author remains unknown.

Who is the Mexican mouse cartoon?

Speedy Gonzales is a Mexican mouse cartoon from the cartoon Looney Tunes and Merrie Melodies. He is known as the fastest mouse in all of Mexico.

What is the most popular cartoon in Mexico?

The most famous cartoon in Mexico is El Chavo del Ocho. The first series was released in 2006. It is about an eight-year-old orphan named El Chavo and his friends in their humble neighbourhood.
