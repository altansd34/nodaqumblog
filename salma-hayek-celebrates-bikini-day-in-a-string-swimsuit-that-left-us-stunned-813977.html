Salma Hayek Celebrates Bikini Day in a String Swimsuit That Left Us Stunned

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, August 7, 2024

The 56-year-old actress honored National Bikini Day as she flaunted her famous curves in a super revealing bikini. And even though she is considered to have one of the most desirable figures, Hayek admitted that she is often tempted to let herself go.

Salma is the Bikini Queen.

Salma shared with her over 25 million Instagram followers a gorgeous photo showing her wearing a colorful and patterned bikini while she stood in front of a swimming pool. She accessorized her look with long, golden earrings, while she wore her wavy hair slicked back.

She added the caption, ’’Happy #NationalBikiniDay! Can you believe the bikini has only been around for 77 years?! Let’s hope they don’t ban them too #notathrowback.’’ She also wrote the same message in her native Spanish language.

She thrives in the sun.

This summer, the star had been often posting snaps of her wearing bikinis that show her perfectly toned body. Last month, she shared a photo of her chilling at the side of a boat while wearing a gray two-piece.

Next to the pic, she wrote ’’Some people don’t like gray days, but I think every day is precious. Adore your week, no matter what it brings ❤️’’

Looking this good doesn’t come easy for her.

The House of Gucci actress has openly discussed her commitment to maintaining a remarkable physique well into her fifties, along with the obstacles she has encountered during the natural aging process.

During an interview in 2017, she admitted that it may be tempting to relax a little, but her desire to remain appealing to her spouse, François-Henri Pinault, a French businessman, keeps her motivated.
She admitted, ’’I will sometimes say, ’I am 50 years old! Why do I have to look good? I already got my guy!’’’ She added ’’But then, I don’t want to lose the guy, either.’’

We can’t stop admiring Salma for her positive attitude and her honesty. She even once revealed the ’’disgusting’’ secret that allows her to keep her stunning physique.
