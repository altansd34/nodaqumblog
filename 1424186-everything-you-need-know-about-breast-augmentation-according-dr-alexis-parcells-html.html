Everything You Need to Know About Breast Augmentation: According to Dr Alexis Parcells

Posted by Kelle Repass on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

In recent years, breast augmentation continues to remain one of the most popular cosmetic procedures. However, not everyone fully understands what breast augmentation is and what is involved in the procedure.

Dr Alexis Parcells is a board-certified plastic surgeon, clinical instructor, and author of several textbooks and professional journal articles. Her expertise includes, but is not limited to, non-surgical facial rejuvenation and resurfacing, cosmetic surgery, breast augmentation, breast lifts, breast reconstruction, and liposuction.

As a surgeon trained in the most cutting-edge plastic surgery techniques, Dr Parcells utilizes a combination of the most advanced surgical and nonsurgical tools and techniques in order to achieve natural and beautiful outcomes for her patients.

Known for her expertise as a plastic surgeon, Dr Parcells treats and educates her patients on the latest information regarding various procedures. Breast augmentation is surgery that aims to enhance a woman’s breast size and shape. Each person is unique and her goals are different. Dr Parcells spends a significant amount of time getting to know each patient before proceeding with breast augmentation surgery.

Sometimes, breast augmentation is not the best solution. Dr Alexis Parcells (@alexisparcellsmd) stresses the importance of having a consultation with a board-certified plastic surgeon to learn about all procedures available to achieve your desired results. The consultation helps you know whether you are a good candidate and educate you on the risks involved and the healing process.

While breast augmentation involves implants, Dr Parcells says very few people know the different types of implants and the difference in quality. To avoid surgical complications, you should consider having your breast augmentation surgery done by a highly qualified and reputed board-certified plastic surgeon. She also stresses the value of keeping your expectations realistic. Analyzing the procedure with the plastic surgeon helps you know what’s achievable and give a clear picture of the expected outcome.

Dr Parcells understands that every woman’s body is unique and so the recovery process differs for every woman. Dr Alexis Parcells loves providing all the information she can to her patients, which helps them make informed decisions for themselves. She believes that every woman has the right to decide what is best for her and by providing the information that is needed to make a good decision, she is continuing to empower more women.
