Cher, 76, Makes a Red Carpet Debut With 37-Year-Old BF and the Photos Are Too Hot to Handle

Posted by Tandra Barner on Friday, August 9, 2024

Cher and her younger boyfriend Alexander Edwards couldn’t keep their hands off each other as they appeared as an official couple for the first time during the 2023 Versace show. Based on how loved up they looked together, it seems like the famous singer has finally found her soulmate. Keep reading to learn what she has to say about her new lover.

MICHAEL TRAN/AFP/East News

Cher and Alexander have reportedly been an item since November 2022. The 76-year-old singer realizes that their almost 40-year age gap has raised a few eyebrows, but, according a source close to her, Alexander “feels special.” And now, we all got the opportunity to see just how special he really is to Cher.

IMAGO/Faye Sadou/Imago Stock and People/East News

After several months of keeping their romance private, the lovebirds decided it was time to show the world what a great match they are. The couple chose the March 2023 Versace fashion show to debut their relationship and managed to steal the entire spotlight.

IMAGO/Faye Sadou/Imago Stock and People/East News, MICHAEL TRAN/AFP/East News

Cher kept close to Alexander as they were all smiles on the red carpet. The 2 even shared a few kisses, which made even our hearts beat a bit faster. Dare we say that the iconic performer looked so happy with her new beau, she reminded us of a young teenager in love.

A few months ago, Cher opened up about her new relationship, revealing exactly why her and Alexander are such a good match. She said, “Well, on paper, it’s kind of ridiculous, but in real life, we get along great. He’s fabulous, and I don’t give men qualities that they don’t deserve, you know? But he’s very, very kind, he’s very smart, he’s very talented, and he’s really funny. And I think he’s quite handsome.”

In case you want to know more about Alexander Edwards, we got you. Cher’s new boyfriend is a music executive, who works closely with artists like Post Malone, Tyga, Ariana Grande, and more. He has never been married, but has a 3-year-old son named Slash Electric.
