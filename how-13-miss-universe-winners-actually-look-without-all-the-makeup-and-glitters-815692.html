How 13 Miss Universe Winners Actually Look Without All the Makeup and Glitters

Posted by Billy Koelling on Saturday, August 10, 2024

All the Miss Universe winners possess remarkable beauty, but beyond their crowns, each of them is crafting a unique and equally inspiring life journey. As they offer glimpses into their daily lives to their millions of followers, we get to see the diverse personalities of these women. This also enables us to witness them showcasing their natural beauty without makeup and in everyday clothing.

Miss Universe 2009 — Stefanía Fernández, Venezuela

Miss Universe 2010 — Ximena Navarrete, Mexico

Miss Universe 2011 — Leila Lopes, Angola

Miss Universe 2012 — Olivia Culpo, United States

Miss Universe 2013 — Gabriela Isler, Venezuela

Miss Universe 2014 — Paulina Vega, Colombia

Miss Universe 2015 — Pia Wurtzbach, Philippines

Miss Universe 2016 — Iris Mittenaere, France

Miss Universe 2017 — Demi-Leigh Tebow, South Africa

Miss Universe 2018 — Catriona Gray, Philippines

Miss Universe 2019 — Zozibini Tunzi, South Africa

Miss Universe 2020 — Andrea Meza, Mexico

Miss Universe 2021 — Harnaaz Sandhu, India

Like these beauty queens, many famous women share photos of themselves without any makeup, serving as a reminder that the radiance of natural beauty is unparalleled.
